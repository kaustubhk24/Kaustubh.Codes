Que > Program to demonstrate the use of math module and use of its various mathematical functions.

```python title="file.py"

print(math.pi)
print(math.e) #Euler's number
print(math.log(10))
print(math.exp(10))
print(math.e**10)
print(math.sqrt(9))
```
